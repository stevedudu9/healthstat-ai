Overview

A working multi-dataset version of HealthStat AI.

One analytics platform, many healthcare datasets.

HealthStat AI reads the uploaded dataset, detects the likely disease context, finds the outcome column, profiles clinical variables, builds a reusable prediction workflow, and adapts the dashboard automatically.

Research & Educational Use Only This application is intended for educational and analytical purposes. It is not a medical device, should not be used for diagnosis or treatment decisions, and has not undergone clinical validation.
