# HealthStat AI Demo Video Script

Target length: 2 to 3 minutes.

## Video Title

HealthStat AI: Adaptive Healthcare Risk Prediction and Statistical Analytics Platform

## Opening

"In this demo, I will show HealthStat AI, a healthcare analytics platform that adapts to multiple datasets instead of being limited to one disease prediction task."

"The application is designed as a portfolio prototype for healthcare data analysis, statistical modeling, and explainable machine learning. It is for research and educational use only, not for diagnosis or treatment decisions."

## Scene 1: Home Page and Disclaimer

Show the home page.

Say:

"The first thing users see is a clear research and educational use disclaimer. This is important for healthcare software because the model has not undergone clinical validation."

"The platform supports several demo workflows: heart disease prediction, diabetes risk prediction, breast cancer classification, and stroke risk prediction."

## Scene 2: Dataset Detection

Click "Load Dataset" or go to "Data Intake."

Say:

"I can choose from demo datasets or upload a CSV file. Each dataset has different column names and structures."

Click each demo briefly.

Say:

"The app automatically detects the healthcare context based on clinical column patterns. It also detects the outcome column, such as `target`, `Outcome`, `diagnosis`, or `stroke`."

## Scene 3: Exploratory Analysis

Open "Exploratory Analysis."

Say:

"The exploratory analysis module summarizes data quality, missing values, duplicate rows, numeric variables, and feature relationships with the outcome."

"This gives the user a quick statistical overview before looking at model predictions."

## Scene 4: Risk Prediction

Open "Risk Prediction."

Say:

"The app trains a lightweight logistic regression model directly in the browser. It reports validation accuracy, precision, recall, F1 score, and ROC-AUC where available."

"The risk simulator lets a user adjust patient features and calculate an estimated probability. The explanation shows which variables are increasing or decreasing the model score."

## Scene 5: Explainable AI

Open "Explainable AI."

Say:

"The explainability section ranks the top risk factors based on their relationship with the outcome. This makes the model more transparent and easier to discuss in a healthcare analytics setting."

## Scene 6: Report

Open "Clinical Report."

Say:

"Finally, the app generates a clinical-style analytical report. The report includes the detected dataset type, data quality summary, model validation metrics, top risk factors, and limitations."

## Closing

"The key idea is that HealthStat AI is not a single-dataset model. It is a reusable healthcare analytics platform that adapts to different disease datasets."

"Future improvements would include larger public datasets, cross-validation, calibration analysis, external validation, and clinical expert review."

## Short Portfolio Pitch

"HealthStat AI demonstrates healthcare analytics, statistical modeling, logistic regression, explainable AI, dashboard design, and responsible communication of model limitations."
